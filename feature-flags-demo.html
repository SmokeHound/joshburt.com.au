How to Use

  1. Go to Settings page
  2. Scroll to the "Feature Flags" section
  3. Toggle any of the three feature flags (Beta Features, New Dashboard, Advanced Reports)
  4. Click "Save Settings"
  5. Return to this page and click "Check All Flags" to see the changes
  6. The corresponding sections will show/hide based on your settings
